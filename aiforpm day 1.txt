RCTCF (role,content,task,*constraints,formatting)

act as an elite enterprise project management consultant. 
i will provide you with raw notes for a new project.
i need you to generate a comprehensive, professional Project Charter Draft.
use a clean hierarchical structure with the following sections:
1. project identification (name, PM:Loren, Sponsor:Pia, Key Stakeholder:Sonny)
2. Business Case & Objectives (include measurable KPIs based on the goals)
3. project scope (in-scope vs out-scope boundaries based on goals)
4. high-level milestone schedule (map out 6 months logically)
5. high-level risks and constraints (infer 3-4 realistic risks based on technology stack)

activity 7: WBS generation

generate a strictly formatted, multi-level work breakdown structure

p:
act as a technical project manager. 
I am managing  the rollout of a new physical server room in our Manila office.
create a detailed work breakdown structure (WBS) down to Level 3 
(Project -> Deliverable -> Work Package)

focus specifically on these three major phases:
1. Infrastructure Preparation (HVAC, Power, Cabling)
2. Hardware Installation (Racks, Servers, Switches)
3. Testing and handover

present the output as a nested numbered list. Do not include Software configuration.

activity 8: identify scope creep in stakeholder communication

p:
act as a strict project management office auditor.

here is our approved scope baseline for the "Website Redesign Project":
- update homepage UI
- migrate existing blog posts to new CMS
- optimize images for mobile viewing

here is an email just received from stakeholder lito:
"hi team, the homepage ui looks great! while we are at it, can we also
quickly rebuild the user login portal to match the new colors, and maybe 
add a chat widget on the bottom right? Thanks!"

task:
1. identify any elements in lito's email that constitues scope creep.
2. draft a professional, diplomatic response email from the PM acknowledging
the ideas but explaining the change control process and how these will affect
the current timeline.
